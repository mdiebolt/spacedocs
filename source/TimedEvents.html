every(n, fn)

      

        
Execute fn every n frames.

        

          
Usage

          
player = GameObject()
          
          player.include TimedEvents
          
          # doSomething is called every 4 frames
          player.every 4, ->
            doSomething()

  • 
              n
               -
              Number of frames to wait before executing the callback

  •
